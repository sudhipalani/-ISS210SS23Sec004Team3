Mental Illness is more common than you think

1 in 5 Americans will experience a mental illness in a given year

Posted by Marley Elsemore on April 28, 2023

When most people think of mental illness they normally don’t think it is as common as it is. According to the Children’s HopeChest, about one in four people worldwide will be affected by a mental health issue. This issue will only get worse as time goes on if they don’t decide to get help.

Because most people do not realize how important it is to understand and accept mental health issues, a lot of them go undiagnosed or the ones who suffer do not get help or the right resources to restore their mental health. According to Turnbridge about two thirds of mental health problems go undiagnosed every year.

Some common mental health issues many around you may suffer from could be anxiety, depression, eating disorders and many more. It is important that as a society and a community we do our best to recognise these issues around us and either point someone in the right direction or if possible give them resources to help.
