How does Mental illness affect variety of life such as relationships?

Posted by Sudhiksha Palanichamy on April 28, 2023

Mental Health is more common thank you think and even though it is an invisible condition it greatly affects our everyday relationships. Sometimes our mental health symptoms can make us feel lethargic, impact our ability to express empathy, or cause feelings of anxiety and isolation. Sometimes these symptoms can cause codependency or even resentment of your partner.

A common Mental illness is anxiety. Anxiety can put a great strain on relationships, such as friendships and relationships, and results in isolation. Many people with anxiety isolate themselves out of fear of things going wrong. Family and friends may feel strained or stressed by their loved one’s anxiety and don’t want them to suffer. It can also be confusing for them if you ignore them because of your anxiety.
