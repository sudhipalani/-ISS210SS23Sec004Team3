Mentall illness Treatment or Lack Thereof/h1>

There are many barries that prevent people with Mental illness from accessing treatment.

Posted by Holly Betram on April 28, 2023

Mental Health Treatment

Many Americans have reported needing mental health help. Many of these Americans also reported to not receiving any treatment. There are a multitude of explanations for this lack of help.

National Survey on Drug Use and Health.

As seen above, some of the top reasons why mental health help is missed out is cost, thinking they can handle the issue themselves, did not know where to go, and did not have time. These issues have been rising from 2008-2018.

“In 2018, about 5.8% of American adults believed they had an unmet need for mental health services during the past year—up from 4.7% in 2015”

Some other statistics show that the rise was steepest between the ages of 18-25; 5.1% in 2015 to 12.7% in 2018. With affordability constantly a top issue, there has to be a way to bring these numbers down and fix this problem.
